FAQ
How do I use the random recipe finder?
Simply click the button in our home page and it will redirect you to a randomly recipe. Our database have 200+ recipes, so don't feel guilty on clicking on it too much!
What is the purpose of this website?
Are you bored at your home and wants to know how to cook? Our website is the perfect option for you then! "Hey, Wanna Cook?" makes you learn different recipes from around the world in a funny and mysterious way.
Are the recipes in the generator of your own?
No. We use another website API which also publishes recipes from all around the globe. If you want to know the site which supports our database, feel glad to send us an email and we will send you the site.
How can I suggest new ideas and filters?
Any suggestions are greatly appreciated. We always want to improve "Hey, Wanna Cook?" and we are open-minded to whatever comes to your mind. Just drop us an email here and we'll take on board what you've got to say.
